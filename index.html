What is the crosswind calculator?
The crosswind calculator is a tool that calculates the crosswind, headwind, and tailwind components of the blowing wind. How to calculate crosswind?
To calculate crosswind you should use the following formula:
crosswind component = wind speed * sin(wind direction)
Why use crosswind calculator?
The crosswind calculator is used in order to measure the cross, head and tail wind components of the blowing wind.
